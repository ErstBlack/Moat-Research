## Mission

Continuously archive every NOTAM (Notice to Air Missions) issued in the FAA's
flight information region — GPS-jamming/spoofing exercise corridors, runway
closures, military-operations-area activations, VIP TFRs, drone restrictions,
navaid outages, obstacle hazards, laser/light shows. Each NOTAM has a defined
effective period; once it expires or is cancelled, the FAA NOTAM Search tool
ceases to return it, and FAA's historical-NOTAM lookup (the only public
retrospective channel) is partial, lags by months, and operational-detail
fields can be redacted on retrospective release per FAA Order JO 7930.2. A
continuous capture preserves full effective-period metadata, original
operational-detail text, and cancellation timestamps that the live feed
exposes but no public archive durably retains. The moat is the historical
record: a 2026 OSINT analyst, a 2027 aviation-insurance underwriter, or a
2028 academic-aviation-safety researcher cannot reconstruct the 2026
GPS-denial-exercise footprint from the FAA's historical lookup at the
fidelity the live feed exposed.

## Capture scope

- **What:** Three-tier capture: (1) full-feed poll of notamSearch backend at >=30s cadence (~2 req/min) to detect new/expired/cancelled NOTAMs by ID set delta; (2) airport-by-airport detail crawl at >=5s minimum gap between successive airport requests (a single sequential worker, ~12 req/min upper bound, ~28h per full sweep across the ~20k US airports) for full operational-detail text; (3) optional registered NOTAM API fallback (api.faa.gov free token; operator registered 2026-05-05 awaiting approval, no TOS surfaced on the registration flow — Tier-3 service deferred until approval response is received and any presented terms (or the documented absence of terms) are captured into docs/api-faa-gov-terms.md in the new project repo) for any feed elements the public UI underexposes. All three flows emit append-only JSON event records keyed on (NOTAM_ID, observation_timestamp). **Capture is type-agnostic across all NOTAM Q-codes (QXXXX subject/scope codes) and free-text NOTAMs — no allowlist, no denylist, no Q-code filtering at any tier including Tier-1 ID-set delta detection.** ICAO Q-code taxonomy for GPS-interference / GNSS-unreliable / GPS-testing is not stable across authorities or time (FAA practice has shifted at least once in the past five years; ICAO's NOTAM Office of Primary Responsibility periodically revises the code list), and a non-trivial fraction of GPS-denial NOTAMs describe interference in free text without carrying a canonical Q-code. Pre-classifying at ingest would silently drop NOTAMs the taxonomy doesn't currently recognize, split the corpus by date across taxonomy revisions, and bake today's filter limitation into a permanent archive (a §5 self-inflicted wound — same failure shape as throwing away body text). Q-code-based selection happens only at the derived-artifact / query layer, against the raw archive. **Raw NOTAM body text is captured verbatim — no parsing, no field extraction, no chain resolution at capture time.** ICAO NOTAMs are cancel-and-replace (NOTAMC issues a new ID; there is no in-place amendment), so the only durable record of a REPLACES-chain lives in the body text of successor NOTAMs; throwing away or pre-parsing the body at ingest would make later chain reconstruction unreconstructible from this archive. Iteration 1 explicitly defers REPLACES-chain extraction, structured-field parsing, and Q-code taxonomy enforcement to a later derived-artifact (Lane-4) layer built against the raw archive. Single Docker Swarm service per tier; throttle coordinator caps aggregate FAA load at 10 req/min.
- **Retention:** Append-only JSON events stored under /mnt/faa-notams/events/YYYY/MM/DD/<batch>.jsonl.gz. Parquet rollups for cross-period queries (NOTAMs active during window W; NOTAMs cancelled within N hours of issuance; NOTAMs whose published scope changed mid-validity). No hot-tier database for capture; DuckDB or TimescaleDB for derived analytics.
- **Derived artifacts:** Active-NOTAM corpus snapshots at hourly granularity for any historical instant in the archive period, REPLACES-chain reconstruction across NOTAM IDs (ICAO NOTAMC cancel-and-replace, parsed from successor body text — derived from raw-body archive, not in-place amendment), GPS-denial / spoofing-exercise footprint atlas (geo-polygon + date-range corpus filtered on JO-7930.2 GPS interference NOTAM types), Special-use-airspace activation timeline per MOA / restricted area / warning area, VIP TFR corpus with issuance lead-time distributions (politically-tracked), Drone-restriction NOTAM corpus for delivery-platform corridor planning
